Wedding Decoration Cost Guide for Trichy "” What to Expect in 2026

Planning your wedding budget can feel overwhelming. This guide provides a realistic overview of decoration costs in Tiruchirappalli.

Understanding Pricing

Wedding decoration costs vary based on scale, flower selection, venue size, and design complexity. Here is what you can expect in 2026.

Essential Tier: 25,000 "” 50,000

A simple yet elegant setup with a basic stage backdrop, standard florals, entry decoration, and basic lighting. Ideal for intimate family weddings with 200 to 300 guests.

Premium Tier: 50,000 "” 1,20,000

Custom stage design, premium flowers (roses, orchids, lilies), enhanced lighting, entrance design, manavarai decoration, and bridal garlands. Suits 400 to 600 guest celebrations.

Luxury Tier: 1,20,000 and above

Bespoke stage designs, imported exotic flowers, crystal chandeliers, full venue transformation, multiple ceremony designs, and a dedicated design coordinator.

Key Cost Factors

  • Imported flowers cost significantly more than locally sourced ones
  • Larger venues require more materials and labour
  • Peak season (April to June) may carry premium rates
  • Custom designs and intricate details require more craftsmanship
  • Multi-day weddings need separate setups
