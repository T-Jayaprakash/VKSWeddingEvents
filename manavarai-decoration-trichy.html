Sacred Ceremony

Honouring Tradition with Grace

The manavarai is the heart of a South Indian wedding "” the sacred canopy where two lives become one. At VKS, we treat this space with the reverence it deserves while adding our signature touch of refined beauty.

Fresh banana stems, mango leaf torana, jasmine garlands, and sacred brass lamps "” every element is sourced with care and arranged with precision. Whether you prefer a traditional temple-style setup or a contemporary floral canopy, we bring it to life with warmth and artistry.

When the bride and groom sit beneath a VKS manavarai, they feel the tradition, the love, and the beauty all at once.
