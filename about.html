Our Story

Crafting Grand Celebrations Since 2012

VKS Decorations was founded with a singular vision: to bring world-class luxury wedding decor to Tiruchirappalli and surrounding districts. What started as a small family-run decoration business has grown into one of Trichy's most trusted premium event styling companies.

Over 12 years, we have decorated more than 500 weddings, receptions, engagements, and corporate events. Our team of skilled artisans and designers blend traditional South Indian aesthetics with contemporary luxury to create unforgettable experiences.

From grand royal wedding stages adorned with fresh jasmine and roses, to authentic manavarai setups with banana stems and sacred elements, we handle every detail with precision and care. Our commitment to on-time execution and premium quality has earned us the trust of hundreds of families across Tamil Nadu.
