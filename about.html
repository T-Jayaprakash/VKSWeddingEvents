Who We Are

We Are Obsessed with Perfection

We are not just another decoration company. We are a team of designers, floral artists, and event architects who lose sleep over making sure every single petal, every light, every drape is exactly right. That obsession is what separates a good event from one that gives you goosebumps.

The Beginning

From a Small Dream to Trichy's Finest

VKS Garland and Decorations started in 2012 with one man, one vision, and a deep love for creating beautiful spaces. What began as a small family-run floral service has grown into one of Tiruchirappalli's most sought-after event design studios.

Today, we handle everything "” grand wedding stages, intimate birthday setups, high-profile political functions, corporate inaugurations, and bespoke floral bouquets. No matter the scale, we bring the same fire and dedication to every single project.

Our clients don't just hire us. They trust us. They come back to us. They recommend us to everyone they know. And that trust "” built over 12+ years "” is the foundation of everything we do.
