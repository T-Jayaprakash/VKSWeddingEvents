Who We Are

About VKS Garland and Decorations

VKS Garland and Decorations is known for creating beautiful and memorable event experiences across Tiruchirappalli and Tamil Nadu. What started as a small floral decoration service has now grown into one of the most trusted names in wedding and event decoration in Trichy.

At VKS Garland and Decorations, every event is treated as a unique celebration. Our team specialises in wedding general decoration, traditional manavarai setups, entrance arch design, garlands and floral maalai, birthday celebrations, corporate events, and complete venue transformations. We bring the same passion and obsessive attention to detail to a first birthday party as we do to the grandest wedding reception.

Families trust VKS Garland and Decorations because we combine traditional craftsmanship with modern design sensibility to create breathtaking event spaces. Our deep roots in South Indian wedding culture mean we understand the rituals, the aesthetics, and the emotions behind every celebration we design.

Born in Trichy. Trusted across Tamil Nadu. Over 500 events completed. Over 12 years of mastery. VKS Garland and Decorations is not just a decoration service — we are the team that makes your most important moments look exactly as beautiful as you imagined them.

Our Journey

From a Small Dream to Trichy's Most Trusted

VKS Garland and Decorations started in 2012 with one man, one vision, and a deep love for creating beautiful spaces. What began as a small family-run floral service has grown into one of Tiruchirappalli's most sought-after event design studios.

Today, we handle everything — grand wedding stages, intimate birthday setups, high-profile political functions, corporate inaugurations, and bespoke floral garlands. No matter the scale, we bring the same fire and dedication to every single project. Our clients don't just hire us — they trust us, they come back, and they recommend us to everyone they know.

Our Services

What Makes VKS Garland and Decorations Different

Each service we offer is delivered with the same obsessive attention to detail that has made us Trichy's most trusted name in event design.

Wedding Stage Design

VKS Garland and Decorations creates stunning wedding stages that become the visual highlight of your entire event — from grand reception backdrops to sacred mandap designs.

Floral Garlands

Traditional and modern garlands crafted with fresh flowers daily by VKS Garland and Decorations. Every garland is strung on the morning of your event, never stored overnight.

Manavarai Setup

Sacred ceremony canopies crafted with reverence — banana stems, mango leaves, jasmine garlands, and lamp arrangements that honour your traditions beautifully.

Entrance Arch Design

From weddings to corporate inaugurations, VKS Garland and Decorations designs entrance arches that make a powerful first impression and set the tone for your event.

Birthday & Celebrations

Every milestone deserves to feel magical. VKS Garland and Decorations designs first birthdays, baby showers, engagements, and anniversary celebrations with the same passion as the grandest weddings.

Corporate & Political Events

Professional event spaces that project authority and respect. VKS Garland and Decorations delivers stage design for inaugurations, conferences, award functions, and political events with flawless execution.
